About

Share and Care

Welcome to Share and Care, a platform dedicated to fostering generosity and compassion through donations. Our mission is to connect donors with recipients in need, creating a community where individuals can support each other.

Our web application features three main sections:

  1. Donor: This section allows individuals to contribute daily used products or any other items they wish to donate.
  2. Recipient: Here, those in need can directly approach donors and request assistance.
  3. Admin: The admin section manages and oversees the donation process, ensuring transparency and efficiency.

At Share and Care, we believe that small acts of kindness can make a significant difference in people's lives. Whether you're giving or receiving, we're here to facilitate a culture of sharing and caring.
