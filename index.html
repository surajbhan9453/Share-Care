Share & Care - Foundation

Share & Care is a revolutionary online donation app designed to bridge the gap between those willing to give and those in need. With a user-friendly interface and a seamless process, it empowers individuals to extend a helping hand by donating their daily used items, including clothes, food, household essentials, shoes, and even watches, directly to those who require them the most..
